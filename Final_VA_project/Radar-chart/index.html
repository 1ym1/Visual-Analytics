State selection:

This Radar chart allows us to visualize the average annual student debt per field of study for each of the states of the USA. You can select one or more states at once to compare their debt distribution. We propose the "All States" option to compare the average debt of the USA with specific states. The associated color of a state is indicated by a colored dot next to the state name and checkbox.
